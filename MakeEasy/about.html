Binary Plan

A Binary Plan members a Two Legged(Left Leg,Right leg) structure in Network Marketing where each new distributor or members is placed in either left or right subtree.One subtree is known as a Power Leg or Profit Leg while the second subtree is a Profit Leg or a weak Leg.

An effective binary compenstation plan gives value to all members and the best part is that the plan comes with an "equality" orinted approach. Active pepole in any industry will gain with great ease.Based up on MLM leads new distributors are placed on the left and right leds,thus the tree grows on and on.Also,a well-balanced system makes the plan active.
